Neptune Mutual
Association

The purpose of the Neptune Mutual Association is to moderate the Neptune Mutual marketplace and oversee the governance of the incident reporting and voting process.

Neptune Mutual Association does not participate in commercial transactions within the marketplace and is run as a not-for-profit organisation. For more information about the governance of Neptune Mutual Association, please reach out to us.

Founded in 2022, Neptune Mutual Association is domiciled in Zug, Switzerland.
